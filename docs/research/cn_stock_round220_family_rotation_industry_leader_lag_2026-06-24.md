## Why This Family

The thesis is public and interpretable: liquid industry leaders may incorporate information first, and same-industry laggards may adjust with a delay. The research target is not plain industry relative strength. It must be tested as within-industry leader-laggard diffusion after controlling for each stock's own momentum/reversal, industry, size, liquidity, volatility, and public technical references.
