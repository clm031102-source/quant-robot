## Enforced Method Areas

Round266 requires every future candidate plan to declare and later evidence these areas before any profitability claim:

- A-share real tradeability: limit-up/down, suspension, ST, listing age, delisting risk, board permission.
- Financial PIT timing: announcement or revision availability date, lag, duplicate keys, stale signals, no period-end leakage.
- Industry/style neutralization: industry, size, value, low-vol, momentum, liquidity, residual matrix, reference overlap.
- CN ETF boundary: stock factors and ETF rotation signals stay separated.
- Portfolio construction: return, annual return, profit rate, Sharpe, cost-adjusted Sharpe, max drawdown, win rate, turnover, capacity.
- Strict statistics: Deflated Sharpe, purged CPCV, White Reality Check or FDR, sensitivity, overlap-adjusted stats, final holdout gate.
- China market regime: policy/liquidity, credit, northbound/margin/turnover temperature, index location, allowed/blocked regime dates.
- Event factors: forecast/statement, dividend/ex-right, buyback/holder/unlock, index rebalance, event contamination.
